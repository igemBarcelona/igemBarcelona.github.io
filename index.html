IGEM Barcelona 2018

Creating bacteria to absorb palmitic acid

Our Project

The iGEM is a international contest where research groups of several countries show their investigations in a collaborative manner.

Recently, the Salvador Aznar Benitah research group of ICREA of Instituto de Investigación Biomédica (IRB Barcelona) discovered that the palmitic acid presence plays a important role in metastasis process. Specifically, the percentage of metastasis in mouse with oral cancer was reduced a 50% changing a high fat diet for a habitual one.

Our proposal is to create a bacteria of the E. coli strain modified to absorb huge quantities of palmitic acid. By this way, we believe that we are able to reduce the probability of metastasis in cancer patients in a noticeable way.
