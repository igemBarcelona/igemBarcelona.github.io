IGEM Barcelona 2018

Creating bacteria to fight metastasis

Our Project

The iGEM is an international competition where research groups all over the world share their projects in a collaborative manner.

Recently, the Salvador Aznar Benitah research group of ICREA of Instituto de Investigación Biomédica (IRB Barcelona) discovered that the palmitic acid expression plays an important role in metastasis process. Specifically, the percentage of metastasis in mouse with oral cancer was reduced a 50% changing a high fat diet for a balanced one.

Our project aimed to design a genetically modified probiotic able to absorb huge quantities of palmitic acid. In this way, we will create a transgenic E.Coli with an enhanced metabolism in fatty acid uptake that could be used to prevent metastasis.
